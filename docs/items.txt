Types of items in Ruinology:

Potions:
	-These are Usable Items and are carried in player's Bag.
	-Effects vary from recovery of health or stamina to doubling effect of player's score.

Money:
	-Money increases player's score.
	-Value varies based on the type of money, or valuable gems or treasures obtained.
	
Hourglass:
	-Are not Usable Items and can work once when picked up.
	-Amt of time extended based on type of hourglass.
